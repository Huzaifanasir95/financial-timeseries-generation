Future Research Directions

Hybrid Architectures

Combine TimeGAN's supervised temporal loss with Diffusion's iterative refinement. Potential for best-of-both-worlds: temporal consistency + high sample quality.

Conditional Generation

Extend TimeGAN with macro-economic conditioning (interest rates, VIX) for scenario-based generation. Critical for stress testing under specific market conditions.

Multi-Asset Modeling

Joint generation of multiple correlated assets. Preserve cross-asset dependencies for realistic portfolio simulations and systemic risk analysis.

Regime-Aware Models

Detect and model different market regimes (bull, bear, high volatility). Generate synthetic data that captures regime-switching dynamics.

Implementation Best Practices

1

Proper Data Preprocessing

Use log returns (not raw prices). MinMax normalize to [0, 1]. Handle missing data carefully.

2

Hyperparameter Tuning

Adjust epochs based on asset volatility: 10K for stocks, 15K+ for crypto. Balance loss weights (λ, γ).

3

Comprehensive Evaluation

Don't rely on KS score alone. Check ACF plots, distribution moments, and statistical tests.

4

Validation Strategy

Use held-out test set from different time periods. Verify generated data performs similarly in downstream tasks.
