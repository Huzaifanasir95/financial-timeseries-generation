Research Overview

Objective

Comprehensive comparative analysis of TimeGAN and Diffusion models for financial timeseries generation across diverse asset classes including equities, indices, and cryptocurrencies. Evaluated on statistical fidelity metrics including KS statistics and mean difference across 12 assets.

Key Innovation

First systematic evaluation comparing temporal GANs with diffusion models on financial data, revealing TimeGAN's superior temporal consistency preservation through supervised step-ahead prediction loss. Statistical significance confirmed via paired t-tests and Wilcoxon signed-rank tests.

Diffusion Limitations
