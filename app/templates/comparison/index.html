Why TimeGAN Wins

  • Supervised Loss: Step-ahead prediction preserves temporal dependencies
  • Latent Space: Learns meaningful temporal representations via embedder
  • Joint Training: Combines reconstruction + supervised + adversarial losses
  • GRU Architecture: Explicitly models sequential dependencies

Why Diffusion Struggles

  • Independent Timesteps: Forward process treats time as independent
  • No Temporal Loss: Transformer doesn't explicitly model autocorrelation
  • Computational Cost: 1000 diffusion steps + 100 inference steps
  • Larger Model: 800K params vs 460K (1.7× more)
